Shirley Temple

Description

A great carbonated choice if you want to avoid alcohol but still party with your friends. Make sure everything is alcohol free.

Ingredients

  • Sprite/7-up/Lemon Lime Soda
  • Grenadine
  • Maraschino Cherries

Prepare The Drink

  1. Pour the soda of choice into a glass over ice.
  2. Add in grendaine. Add however much you want tbh. Lotta sugar.
  3. Stir gently. Or not your choice.
  4. Add cherries. Again however many you want. It's your drink.
  5. Add straw. Or don't.
  6. Drink your creation.
  7. Smile
